  • crossing
    • Computational search of small point sets with small rectilinear crossing number Ruy Fabila-Monroy and Jorge Lopez.

      • In that paper we used the crossing module to find instances of the complete geometric graph \( K_n \) of 75 points with very few crossings. There exists a construction that given a point set with few crossing creates larger and larger sets with few crossings. We were able to break the asymptotic upper bound.
